DJ Profile

Welcome to the digital home of UK based DJ/Producer Craig Dalzell. With a career spanning over 20 years that include 12 summer seasons in Ibiza playing some of the top clubbing venues the island has to offer, winter residencies in Italy, Andorra and a headline appearance at Ministry Of Sound in Egypt to name a few it’s fair to say he’s been around the musical block just a bit.
